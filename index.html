D. Lewis Greenaway

Outgoing and confident, with experience of housing policy, benefits, disability employment and digital engagement.

I have over five years' experience of charity sector and not-for-profit work, over which time I've worked directly with vulnerable service users, benefits claimants, social housing tenants and a wide variety of stakeholders. I have extensive experience negotiating with stakeholders including the Department for Work and Pensions, Welsh Government, housing associations and funders of every stripe. I have a good understanding of benefits, housing and transport policy, particularly that relating to green issues. I have also gained experience of managing projects and surveys. I have excellent writing and public speaking skills, and have been a regular contributor of articles to TPAS Cymru's website, focussing on private rental sector policy and news.
